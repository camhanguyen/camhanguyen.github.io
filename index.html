Ha Nguyen

PhD Student, STEM Teaching & Learning

I study the co-design of technology-supported learning with teachers, students, and community partners.

Publications

A central question for my research is: How can we promote student-centered, equitable learning in STEM through responsive digital environments?

I draw from the fields of learning sciences, learning analytics, and human-computer interactions to answer this question.

(1) I partner with educators and community partners to design interface and computer-supported learning activities, and study design principles that facilitate educators' sensemaking of data about students' learning.

(2) I experiment with ways to systematically process education textual data, using human-algorithmic approaches.

(3) I apply quantitative research methodologies (e.g., quasi-experimental designs for generalized causal inference, process mining, and social network analyses), to understand the impact of instructional interventions on different student populations.
